## 2. Security Policy

### 2.1 Reporting Vulnerabilities

Please report security issues via:

1. **Private disclosure**: Contact maintainers directly
2. **GitHub Security Advisories**: Use the Security tab
3. **Email**: See SECURITY.md for contact info

### 2.2 Response Timeline

| Severity | Response | Resolution |
|----------|----------|------------|
| Critical | 24 hours | 7 days |
| High | 48 hours | 14 days |
| Medium | 1 week | 30 days |
| Low | 2 weeks | 60 days |
